# CCAW: A Crash Course in Academic Writing

## About these materials

These pages contain a series of tips, warnings, and best practices when it comes to academic writing. They were specifically developed to help students in the University of Borås's MAIDI programme (the Master's Programme in Information Science: Digital Environments) write critical essays and other academic writing assignments. To a large extent, these resources bring together lessons learned from years of assessing students' writing assignments in various programmes across the University of Borås. The materials were developed by Wout Dillen, Senior Lecturer in Library and Information Science at the University of Borås.
